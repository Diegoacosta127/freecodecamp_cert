David "Pelón" Stirling

"Pelón" playing for La Dolfina Polo Team in the Abierto Argentino de Polo 2020


Born January 4, 1981, in Young, Río Negro, is an Uruguayan polo player with a 10-goal handicap who currently plays in Argentina for La Dolfina Polo Team. He is considered one of the best polo players in the world and the best polo player in Uruguayan history (see currently ranking here). He is the winningest foreigner in Argentine tournaments, having won, amongst others, three consecutive Argentine Triple Crowns from 2013 to 2015. He also graduated in Business Administration in England.
